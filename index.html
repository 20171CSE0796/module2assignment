TOP 3 IPL TEAMS

MI

It has a mix of youngsters and experienced players led by Rohit Sharma the most successful captain in this league. Although Mumbai is not my favourite team but I have a lot of respect for this franchise. hey surely are the favourites.

CSK

The yellow army makes one of the largest fan bases in the league. CSK has made more appearances in the playoffs when compared to any other side in the Indian Premier League. They also boast the highest win percentage in the history of this league. Chennai defended a 116 run total against Kings XI Punjab in 2009 which is another record.

KKR

Kolkata Knight Riders are always one of the teams to watch out for in the Indian Premier League. The team owned by Shah Rukh Khan always has some big names in the line-up, but that's where KKR fans will be slightly disappointed as no big Indian players are in the team.
